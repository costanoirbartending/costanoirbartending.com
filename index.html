About Us

The Concept

An exclusive experience designed to combine mixology and photography.

What you will experience: sunset aperitivos by the pool, intimate dinners under the stars, or cocktails enjoyed on the deck of your yacht.

From timeless classics to our exclusive signature cocktails, each experience is tailored and delivered to your favourite location — with elegant glassware, fresh ingredients, and an atmosphere of understated luxury.

What we do

Our Services

01 — Private Bartending

World-class craft, at your table

Years of experience across renowned cocktail bars, five-star hotels and upscale venues in Italy and Australia — brought directly to your event.

The cocktail menu is built around your tastes and preferences — how many drinks, which styles, what flavours. Exclusive seasonal signature cocktails alongside timeless classics, all prepared with 100% fresh ingredients, house-made syrups, juices and infusions.


Non-alcoholic options are always available — same care, same quality, same seasonal approach.

02 — Event Photography

Every moment, forever

Every event is guided by a dedicated host — present from the moment of booking, taking care of the menu, the setup and every detail leading up to the evening. Someone to consult, to coordinate with, and to make sure nothing is left to chance.

During the event, that same person is behind the lens — moving through the space quietly, capturing the spontaneous moments: the smiles, the toasts, the atmosphere as it unfolds. Every image professionally edited and delivered to you in high resolution — because some evenings deserve to last forever.

How We Work

Spirits & Bar Options

We offer two different approaches to spirits — both designed to give you full flexibility while ensuring the highest quality.

A — Dry Hire

Your spirits, our craft

You source the spirits — we handle everything else. We provide a full consultation and a precise shopping list, guiding you through exactly what to buy, how much, and where to find it. Ideal if you already have preferred brands or labels in mind.

B — Open Bar

We take care of everything

The most effortless option — we bring it all. Spirits are handpicked by our bartender from a curated selection of premium labels, ranging from refined to top-shelf. Any unopened bottles at the end of the night are yours to keep.

The Details

Beyond the Bar

Event Setup & Styling

Your vision, our execution

Every event comes with a custom printed cocktail menu — designed around the vibe and theme of the evening. The bar and the space are dressed accordingly: flowers, candles, ambient lighting. Small details that shift the feeling of a room entirely.

Fully Mobile Service

We come to you — anywhere

Everything we do is completely transportable. Villa, yacht, rooftop, garden — we travel to the location and set everything up from scratch.

We bring a complete professional bar setup: crystal glassware for every cocktail style, ice and clear ice, and a range of innovative tools — including a vegan foamer for egg-white free cocktails and specialist equipment for clarified preparations and infusions.
