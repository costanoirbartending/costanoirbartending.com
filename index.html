How We Work

Spirits & Bar Options

We offer two different approaches to spirits and alcohol — both designed to give you full flexibility while ensuring the highest quality at your event.

A — Dry Hire

Your spirits, our craft

You source and purchase the spirits yourself — giving you complete control over budget and brands. We guide you through every choice, recommending exactly what to buy, in what quantities, and where to find it.

  • Full spirits consultation & shopping list provided
  • You choose the brands, guided by our expertise
  • Control over quantity and spend
  • Ideal if you already have preferred labels

B — Open Bar

We take care of everything

We source and bring all the spirits — carefully selected from a curated range of premium labels. Every bottle is chosen with the same attention we give to every other detail of your event.

  • Premium spirits selection, handpicked by our bartender
  • A range of spirit qualities available — from refined to top-shelf
  • Any unopened bottles remaining after the event are yours to keep
  • The effortless option — we handle it all
